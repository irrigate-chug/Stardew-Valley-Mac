## 🧠 What is Stardew Valley for Mac?

Stardew Valley is a farming simulation and role-playing game created by ConcernedApe. Players inherit an old farm and transform it into a thriving homestead while building relationships and exploring the world around them.

It is designed to:

* Grow crops and manage farms
* Raise livestock and animals
* Explore mines and collect resources
* Build friendships with villagers
* Complete quests and challenges
* Customize and expand your property
